At The Common Table, we cater to bespoke menus.

We love preparing a variety of styles including breakfasts, brunch, luncheons, afternoon teas, canapés and dinners. Seasonal menus include BBQs & Sharing menus.

We ensure that our ingredients are responsibly sourced and everything in our kitchen is freshly made to order. We also cater to any specific dietary requirements you may have.

He even invested and oversaw operations at Calcutta Street, a contemporary Bengali kitchen in Fitzrovia.

The Common Table. Because we share

A common love for the flavours and colours of the spectrum of ingredients that London offers

A common passion for creative cooking leading to the voluntary association

A common value system of evolved yet inclusive cooking,  derived from our combined ethno-cultural backgrounds, travel and living experiences

A common purpose of creating a culinary commune that unites food lovers to savour and promote culinary creativity and progress
