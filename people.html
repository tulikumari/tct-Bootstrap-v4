After quitting his job as a Finance controller in Cisco Systems, Som powered through the Diplome de Cuisine at Le Cordon Bleu after which he had short stints at at several contemporary kitchens in Central London including Tredwells, Sketch and Ametsa. He even invested and oversaw operations at Calcutta Street, a contemporary Bengali kitchen in Fitzrovia.

His primary interests lie in Contemporary European and Modernist Catalan/Basque styles and still pledges his loyalty to the somewhat obscure French classical cooking.
